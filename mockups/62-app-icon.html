Stroke-weight study (kanagawa + everforest, 256px)

Same torii at 4 stroke weights. The in-app mark uses ~3px on a 44 viewBox = stroke/viewBox ratio ≈ 6.8%. Scaled to 1024 viewBox that'd be 70px. We push beyond that for icon scale.
