CONCEPT 01
Washi Hinomaru日の丸 · 和紙
en's first light theme. Cream washi-paper bg (not pure white — that reads Microsoft), warm sumi-black ink, and the deep hinomaru red as the accent. Reads like a tea-room scroll. Restraint over volume; the red is precious because sparse.
palette: washi cream → sumi → hinomaru
brand mark: filled red disc (sun, not torii)
texture pairing: grain (paper fibers)
tradeoff: first non-dark theme — light theme infrastructure may need a cross-theme audit pass.

CONCEPT 02
Sumi Kyokujitsu墨旭日
Stays in en's dark family. Charcoal-purple bg, vermillion (朱色 shu-iro) accent. The 16 rising-sun rays become an ambient texture at low alpha, modulated by the existing texAmt slider. Quiet at rest, glowing when you turn it up.
palette: sumi-purple → cream-ink → shu-iro
brand mark: sun + 8 rays glyph (replaces torii for this theme)
new texture: "asahi-rays" — conic gradient, becomes 15th texture option
tradeoff: rays texture might be too on-the-nose; could stay subtle by capping max alpha at ~0.10.

CONCEPT 03
Asahi朝日 · sunrise
Not a flag — a flag-atmosphere. Twilight gradient bg from indigo at the top to washi-cream at the horizon. A soft sun disc lives at the seam, behind the tiles, hinted not stated. Tiles get a slight transparency + backdrop-blur, so the sky shows through. Most painterly of the three.
palette: indigo → dusk → cream + sun-vermillion
brand mark: minimalist filled disc
special: tiles use backdrop-blur to let the sky show through
tradeoff: the gradient bg is the most "different" of the three — breaks the flat-bg precedent of every other en theme. Risk: feels gimmicky over time. Reward: feels alive.
