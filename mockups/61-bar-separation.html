en — bar separation
titlebar + foot · 8 variants · rose-pine-soft canvas
The chrome bars currently fade into the body via a soft --ink-4 gradient. On screen they read more "blended" than "distinct," and macOS WKWebView's compositor leaks subtle translucency through the window edges (decorations: false trade-off). These 8 variants explore ways to give the top + bottom bars more visual identity without breaking the lifted-bg recipe or relying on translucency tricks. Each variant shows the full sandwich: titlebar → canvas → foot. Pick the one that feels like en. Skill-chips label who designed each variant.

Keeps the current ink-wash gradient but adds two subtle architectural lights: a 1px white-sheen inside the bar's edge nearest the canvas (reads as a top-of-material highlight), and an 8–14px soft drop-shadow falling into the canvas (the bar casts a shadow → it's a higher plane). Border darkens slightly to anchor. Reads as: "the chrome is a foreground layer." Fully token-driven, no theme overrides needed.
