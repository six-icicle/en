The cold axis. Each variant shifts three things off the warm original: bg moves from warm-brown (H~25°) toward cool-slate (H~220°); ink moves from cream-tan to neutral/cool grey; accent stays red but desaturates and cools slightly so it doesn't burn against the colder bg.

Tsuki is the most conservative — almost neutral, just whispers cold. Hagane is the obvious cold sibling — clearly slate-blue, still restrained. Yuki goes furthest into the cold — deepest blue undertone, coldest ink.
