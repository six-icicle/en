Reading the matrix. Surfaces (BG / TILE / TILE-2) get progressively lighter on dark themes, progressively darker on the one light theme (washi). Ink hierarchy (INK → INK-4) shifts from primary text to nearly invisible. Accent is the brand axis; OK is the "all good" indicator (working tile dot, status); WARN is the destructive intent (modal-confirm hover fills with this).

The 11th theme — washi-kyokujitsu shares Washi Hinomaru's palette exactly; it differs only in the kyokujitsu rays SVG rendered behind the chrome and the brand-mark glyph. It's the hidden easter-egg theme, surfaced only by renaming a tile to war, wartime, kyokujitsu, asahi, hinode, etc.

Light vs dark cells. Transparency-bearing tokens (LINE / LINE-2 / GLOW) sit on a checkered backdrop so the alpha channel is visible. Solid tokens render straight.
